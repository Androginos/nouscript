NouScript ALPHA

NouScript is an experimental research project designed to redefine how we consume and synthesize information from video and audio content across platforms. By leveraging the state-of-the-art Hermes-4-70B model from Nous Research, we provide deep-reasoning summaries and high-precision translations from YouTube videos, X (Twitter) posts, and recorded Twitter Spaces.

Please note that this platform is currently in Active Development (Alpha). It is intended strictly for research, learning, and developmental purposes. As we continue to refine the in-memory processing and reasoning capabilities, your feedback is invaluable to us.

Connect & Collaborate

We are a community-driven initiative. If you encounter bugs, have feature suggestions, or wish to discuss the underlying architecture, reach out to the developers.

Security & Fair Use

To ensure the stability of the research environment and prevent resource abuse, NouScript employs Cloudflare Turnstile bot protection and a rate limit of 5 queries per hour per user. These measures are strictly for security and fair usage purposes.
